Mentoring at HackExeter

HackExeter needs lots of mentors! Ideally, we will have more mentors than teams. Note that mentorship at HackExeter will be relatively involved; you may be responsible for a team, whom you will have to get through the end of their project no matter what. If a team does not know how to program, you will need to be able to teach prepared lessons on a language that suits their project.

Mentors can be students or teachers of any age, but should have had some experience teaching or tutoring.
