What is HackExeter?

Welcome to the big leagues. In 2015, HackExeter will return as the premiere high school hackathon in the Northeast.

This year, we're bringing the most talented high school developers on the coast for a 24-hour coding marathon filled with coding, workshops, and technical talks from best in the business. We look forward to seeing you here!

Registration for HackExeter is currently closed. Beginners and middle-schoolers are welcome to come to a second event we will hold in the Spring, and we encourage everyone to sign up next year!

Who can attend?

All high school-aged students from any school are welcome. Middle schoolers, stay tuned for an event later in the year!

How much does it cost?

Free ninety nine! Admission is free, food is free, and our giveaways are free for all high school students.

What can I make?

Anything you can build for web, mobile, Pebble, Google Glass, wearables, hardware, etc. is fair game. We'll have a limited supply of hardward hack materials to use as well. Make sure all code you work on was written at the event!

Where there be food?

Yep! We will provide a free dinner, breakfast, and lunch for all attendees, plus late night snacks to keep the hacking up.

How can I register?

Click that button up there! If signups go over capacity, we will hold a registration lottery and update the site with more information.

What should I bring?

Bring a laptop, phone, chargers, and anything else you want to hack on during the event. You're welcome to bring a sleeping bag as well!

Will there be travel reimbursements?

We plan to provide a few coach buses to pick up attendees on the East Coast at certain pickup locations. Stay tuned for updates!

Do I need to know how to code?

No, but getting started before HackExeter might help! We will have workshops and mentors to help you build your first website or app here if you want.

How will teams work?

Work on teams of up to four to build your hack during the event. If you don't have a team, just come a little early and we'll help you find people to work with!
