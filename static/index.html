What is HackExeter?

HackExeter is the place to try something new. Whether you are an artist who has an idea for an app, an experienced programmer who wants to learn Clojure, or a stargazer who thinks that learning to program would be cool, HackExeter is here with mentors, time, facilities, and food to get you through your first project.

FAQ

Who can attend?

All high-school and middle-school aged students are welcome.

How much does it cost?

Nothing! Admission is free, food is free, and our giveaways are free for everyone.

What can I make?

Anything you can demo. Remember, though, HackExeter is about going outside your comfort zone. Try something you don't know how to do!

Will there be prizes?

Absolutely! The teams that tried the hardest things and finished them anyway will win our grand prizes. We will be giving out a total of over $1,800 worth of prizes.

Where there be food?

Yep! We will provide subs for lunch and pizza for dinner. If you have food allergies, email us and we'll try to work something for you.

How can I register?

Click that button up there! You can register individually, with a team, or, if you are a teacher or coach, register a group for your institution. Registration is completely free.

What should I bring?

Bring your laptop and a charger, along with anything else you want to hack on during the event. We may have some extra computers, but don't count on it!

Can I start work beforehand?

Sure! If you're shooting for a prize, though, keep in mind that you will only be scored on the things you added during the event.

How will we be scored?

HackExeter will score you on much more than just your finished product. Our mentors and judges will check in with you periodically, and your time management, teamwork, and sportsmanship will affect your score. You'll recieve some scoring guidelines on contest day.

I can't really code.

Perfect! Come learn. We will be here with plenty of mentors and guidance to get you started and finished. If you make something cool, there's a very good chance you could win!

Do I need a team?

You can work alone, or in a team of up to four people. If you don't have a team, and would like one, come a little early and we'll help you find people to work with!
